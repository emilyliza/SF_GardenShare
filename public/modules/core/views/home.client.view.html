San Francisco Garden Sharing

Fresh local food and beautiful flowers are a vital part of San Francisco's culture and charm. As our population grows, green areas inevitably decrease, making it important to care and cultivate the green space we do have. Bringing together homeowners (with available garden space) with experienced gardeners is a wonderful way to produce food and create community.

Do you have a garden space that is not being used? Would you like to have fresh herbs and maybe a tree or two, but lack the time or know-how? Sign up, post your space on the map and attract a gardener! When you've found a good match, you can arrange a short or long-term plan that is mutually beneficial.

Are you a gardener looking for a place to grow lettuce and potatoes? Check out the map and find a garden space near you. When you see the perfect yard, reach out and make a connection. Great things can happen!
